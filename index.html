Sonant

Play Chance: The probability of playing (and displaying) a note. (0-1)

Mutation Chance: The probability of a note getting changed within a progression. (0-1)

Snap Chance: The probability of a note snapping to a nearby chord note. (0-1)

Max Snap Distance: The maximum distance a chord can snap (1-5)

Octave Range: The octave range of which a note can be placed within. (1-3)

Min Step Distance: The minimum distance the next note moves from the current note. (0-3)

Max Step Distance: The maximum distance the next note moves from the current note. (2-5)

Leap Chance: The probability of placing the next note randomly. (0-1)
